OUR AREA OF EXPERTISE

FULL ARCHITECTURAL SERVICES

Where a client chooses to use our full range of architectural services from start-to-finish of a project, our work is carried out in stages, from preliminary feasibility studies through to the developed technical design and on to the construction and completion of the building works.
We also advise on the need to appoint other appropriate consultants, and on the best way to appoint a contractor to suit the particular requirements of each client and project.

CONCEPT & FEASIBILITY DESIGN

Alongside the preparation of outline proposals to explore and clarify the clients brief, we help to identify the core project requirements and parameters, including cost, planning, sustainability and other regulatory issues.

PLANNING & LISTED BUILDINGS APPLICATIONS

We are highly experienced and successful in dealing with the often complex and sensitive planning process. We apply a knowledgeable and careful approach to the preparation, submission and negotiation of all types of planning and listed building applications, including in conservation areas and for subterranean developments.

INTERIOR DESIGN SERVICED

On projects within the high-end residential sector and for private clients, we offer an in-house interior design service.
This can include the full design of bathrooms and kitchens as well as joinery and lighting design and the selection of finishes and fittings.

SPECIFICATION

Depending on the particular requirements of the project, procurement route and contract, we produce outline specifications and schedules of work up to full NBS specification documents.

These communicate the scope, quality and other requirements of the products and materials that go into making a building project in order to ensure that the contractor delivers what the client is expecting.

Project Management & Contract Administration

Our services in project management and contract administration deal with the coordination necessary to manage the complex process of transforming a brief and design into a building. This includes leading the project team, monitoring the progress and quality of the whole design and construction team against the issues of budget, scheduling and quality control.
We administer the building contract through the procurement, construction, handover and conclusion of the construction process.
